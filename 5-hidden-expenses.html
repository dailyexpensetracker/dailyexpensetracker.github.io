5 Hidden Expenses Leaking Your Monthly Budget

Financial leaks are the silent killers of wealth. They are the $5 to $15 charges that you barely notice on your bank statement, but when added together, they could be costing you thousands of dollars a year.

1. The Subscription Sludge

From streaming services to "pro" versions of apps you rarely use, digital subscriptions are often the biggest leaks. Check your Apple or Google Play subscriptions today.

2. The Convenience Premium

Food delivery apps often charge a 30% markup on food, plus dynamic delivery fees and tips. Moving to a "pickup only" model could save you $200 a month.
