Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 5, 2026

At DailyExpenseTracker.org, we take your financial privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle information when you use our web application, including the "Money Audit" and "Life Energy Traded" features.

1. Our "Privacy-First" Philosophy

Unlike traditional financial apps, DailyExpenseTracker.org is built as a client-side utility. This means that the core functionality of the app—from inputting your "Monthly Income" to generating your "Financial Reality Check"—happens directly within your own web browser.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We believe that your financial data is your business. We operate under these strict protocols:

  • No Server-Side Storage: We do not store your transaction details, income, or budget on our servers.
  • No Personal Accounts: We do not require a login or registration process to use our tool.
  • No Financial Records Access: We never ask for your bank account numbers or sensitive credentials.

3. How Data is Processed

Local Storage: All financial data you enter, such as your "Itemized Breakdown" (e.g., Coffee, Pizza, Uber), is processed locally in your browser to calculate your "Burn Rate" and "Life Energy".

PDF Generation: When you click "Save as PDF Report," the document is generated confidentially within your browser environment.

Streak Tracking: To maintain your "STREAK" (e.g., 1 Days), a small piece of data may be stored in your browser's local storage so the count remains active for your next visit.

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

To keep this tool free and accessible for everyone, we may display advertisements through third-party partners like Google AdSense. These partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Google Ad Settings.

5. Data Security

Because "No data was stored on servers," your financial information is as secure as your own device. Once you close your browser tab or clear your browser's cache, your session data is removed. We recommend that you independently verify all figures before making significant financial decisions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our tool's features or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
